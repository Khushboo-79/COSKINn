# COSKINn Skincare & Makeup Ecommerce Project Documentation

## 1. Project Overview

The project is a skincare and makeup ecommerce platform that will launch
with a limited, premium, fruit-based product range. The business will
include both app and web platforms, supported by backend systems and
internal panels for admin, inventory, HR, audit, tax/finance, customer
support, marketing, and product management.

The project will be inspired by Nykaa's beauty ecommerce model, but it
must not copy Nykaa's brand name, logo, UI, content, images, code, or
exact business identity. The goal is to build an original beauty
commerce platform with a premium but cute brand experience.

Nykaa publicly describes its beauty and personal care model as
predominantly inventory-led, where products are sourced from brands or
authorized distributors to maintain authenticity. A similar approach is
recommended for this project, especially because cosmetics and skincare
require quality control, batch tracking, expiry tracking, and customer
trust.

For COSKINn, the recommended launch model is:

Inventory-led model\
Private label model\
Limited product launch\
Fruit-based skincare and makeup positioning\
App + web ecommerce\
Centralized admin and operations system

The first launch should focus on only selected products instead of
hundreds of products. This will make product development, testing,
branding, inventory, and marketing easier.

# 35. Final Launch Recommendation

For the first launch, COSKINn should work as a private-label,
inventory-led beauty brand instead of a marketplace.

Recommended first launch:

12 skincare products\
12 makeup products\
Customer app\
Customer website\
Admin panel\
Inventory panel\
Order panel\
Payment system\
Shipping system\
Tax invoice system\
Customer support panel\
Marketing panel

Avoid adding marketplace sellers in the first version. Marketplace
seller management will increase complexity because it requires seller
onboarding, seller verification, commission handling, vendor payout,
fake-product control, and additional tax management.

The best first goal is to launch a controlled premium product range,
build trust, collect customer feedback, and then expand slowly into more
products, vendors, and offline channels.
